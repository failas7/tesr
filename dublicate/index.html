Our Dedication

We are most recognized and most popular branded wholesale customer based garment company in Sri Lanka. Undertake any type of sales without depreciating the quality and standards of clothing by also reduced work time targets are achieved with skilled workforce.

Customer suggestions are open heartedly accepted to make future changes in the industry along with the collaboration to Sri Lanka's most governmental biased management panel.

Our Vision

We strive to be a leader in fashion-knit and fashion outerwear by empowering innovation and design to provide total customer satisfaction in Sri Lanka.

We strive to be a caring and well-managed organization for our business partners ,customers and employees, and a responsible corporate citizen to our society.

Our Mission

To innovate, to lead, to enhance, to provide best-value products and services to Sri lankan customers.

To make a difference through our branding to stay ahead of fashion trends, market changes and the latest technology. To enhance the quality of life for our business partners,customers and employees.
