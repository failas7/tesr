F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you believe that your product material is exclusive?

    Yes, Before importing our raw material sample is being brought to have an experiment on durability, and texture quality. After having the experiment and verifying its checkings, we import the materials to Sri Lanka.

  • Why do you sell products with low prices?

    Our mission is to provide quality material products to the best lower prices, according to that we sell our products in a lower price range.

  • How can I wash the clothes?

    For best results, machine washes cotton items in cold or warm water. Do not use hot water as it may shrink the cotton. Use a normal wash cycle and regular detergent (with color-safe bleach if desired). Tumble dry on a low setting, then promptly remove the item from the dryer to avoid wrinkles.

  • Will your clothes wrinkle in size after washing?

    Our products are being washed and cleaned before dispatched to the market.

  • If an item is returned, what are your regulations?

    Normally we don't have many sales returns yet, but in the future, if we get any return goods we would clarify it with our company policy and we will accept it heartily.
